About Zillions

Tending the land like it’s borrowed.

Zillions Landscaping is a small, family-rooted operation in Charlotte, NC. We do the work ourselves — no subcontractors, no shortcuts.

Everything we put down is natural. Real mulch instead of dyed stuff. Compost instead of synthetic fertilizer. We treat each yard like the soil and the people who walk on it actually matter — because they do.

If that sounds like the kind of crew you want on your property, let’s talk.
